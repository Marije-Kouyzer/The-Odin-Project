Curry

Description

This curry with lentils and chickpeas is healthy and delicious.

Follow this recipe to make it yourself.

Ingredients

  • 250 grams of tomatoes
  • 310 grams of chickpeas
  • 400 ml of coconut milk
  • 310 grams of lentils
  • 300 grams of white rice
  • 40 grams of ginger
  • 2 onions
  • garlic
  • coriander
  • pepper
  • curry mixture

Steps

  1. Cut the ginger, onions, garlic and pepper.
  2. Bake the ginger with the onions, garlic and pepper.
  3. Add the curry mixture, tomatoes and chickpeas.
  4. Add the coconut milk. Cook for 10 minutes.
  5. Add the lentils and cook for 5 more minutes.
  6. Add the pepper and salt if you want.
  7. Cook the white rice seperately and serve together.
