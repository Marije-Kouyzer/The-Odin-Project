Empanadas

Description

These delicious empanadas are always a win. They are popular in South America and there are endless variations you can make of them.

Follow the recipe below to make some yourself.

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 1 onion
  • 250 grams of minced meat
  • dough
  • garlic
  • parsley

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven on 200 degrees celcius.
  2. Cook the eggs for 8 minutes.
  3. Cut the onion and the garlic.
  4. Cut the eggs in small pieces and mix them with the minced meat, onion, garlic and parsley.
  5. Put the mixture in the dough.
  6. Bake for 20 minutes.
