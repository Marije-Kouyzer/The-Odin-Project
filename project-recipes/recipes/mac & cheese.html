Mac & Cheese

Description

The following mac and cheese recipe is always a great choice.

Ingredients

  • 300 grams of pasta
  • 500 ml of milk
  • 150 grams of cheese
  • 600 grams of cauliflower
  • garlic
  • nutmeg
  • parsley

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven at 200 degrees celcius.
  2. Cut the cauliflower and and bake in the oven for 15 minutes.
  3. Cook the pasta.
  4. Cut the garlic.
  5. Bake the garlic, and add in the milk slowly.
  6. Add the cheese and nutmeg.
  7. Bake the pasta and and cheese sauce together in the oven for 10 minutes.
  8. Sprinkle the parsley over the pasta.
